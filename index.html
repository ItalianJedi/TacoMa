TACOS ARE THE ANSWER

IT STARTED WITH A TORTILLA...

A taco just isn’t a taco without a good tortilla, so before we ever built our menu we went to work on creating the best hand-made tortilla we possibly could. From there, the inspiration for our menu followed – using fresh ingredients with plenty of vegan and vegetarian items to go along with the classics. We hope you enjoy everything as much as we enjoy making it for you!

LUNCH & DINNER

Authentic Mexican favorites with some twists of our own – featuring diverse international flavors including vegan and vegetarian options..

BRUNCH

Available at our Capitol Hill location, Brunch is served every Saturday & Sunday 11am-3pm. Breakfast tacos, burritos and more plus new boozy drinks from the bar.

MARGS & MORE

Enjoy our ever rotating flavors of traditional and frozen margaritas plus specialty cocktails and all of your favorite beers from Mexico (and local favorites too!).
